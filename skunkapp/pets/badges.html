SkunkApp Pet Badges

Earn special badges for your pet to showcase achievements and status!

Verified Badge

Represented by a Skunk Check Mark, this badge is awarded to pets that:

  • Follow SkunkApp's Pet Guidelines
  • Have at least 10,000 Money
  • Reach Level 10

Popular Badge

Represented by a Skunk Fire icon, this badge is awarded to pets that:

  • Earn 100,000 Money
  • Achieve a minimum of Level 50

Developer Badge

Exclusive to SkunkApp Staff and Admins. This badge signifies:

  • Special access for Developers
  • Adherence to Community Guidelines

Collect badges and let your pets shine in the SkunkApp community!
