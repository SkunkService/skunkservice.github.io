SkunkApp's Pet Guidelines

Welcome to SkunkApp's Pet System of Guidelines. Your pet's safety and well-being are our top priority!

Guidelines:

  1. Pet's Name:

    Names that are inappropriate, bullying, or NSFW (Not Safe For Work) are strictly forbidden. Such names will be ignored during the pet naming process.

  2. Pet's NSFW Content:

    Any NSFW content associated with a pet name is not allowed. This includes any form of explicit material, which will violate our community guidelines.

  3. Pet Safety:

    Ensure that your pet's name reflects a safe and welcoming environment. Names should not incite harm or discomfort within the community.

  4. Pet Suspension:

    Pets may be suspended if there is a violation of the naming guidelines or if any hacking activity is detected.

  5. Pet Hacking:

    Hacking into a pet account is a serious violation of SkunkApp's Pet Guidelines. Any pet account found to be hacked will result in the permanent deletion of all pet data.

  6. Pet Data:

    All pet data will be backed up as part of SkunkApp's data management process. This ensures that your pet's information is safe and secure.

Thank you for helping us maintain a safe and enjoyable environment for all pets on SkunkApp!
