SkunkApp's Events Guidelines

Welcome to SkunkApp’s event community! To maintain a respectful and enjoyable environment, we’ve set the following guidelines for creating, moderating, and participating in events. Please read carefully before engaging in any event activities.

1. Event Creation Guidelines

  • Clear and Accurate Information: Ensure event names, descriptions, start/end times, and other details are accurate and clear.
  • Thumbnail and Media: Event thumbnails should be appropriate and relevant to the event. Avoid using explicit, offensive, or copyrighted content without permission.
  • URL Links: If an event requires a link, ensure it’s safe, accurate, and relevant. Links to inappropriate content will result in event removal and potential account suspension.
  • Pending Approval: Some events may require moderator approval before going live. If your event is pending, please be patient, as approval may take up to 24 hours.

2. Event Moderation

  • Respect Moderator Decisions: Moderators have the authority to approve, reject, or delete events as necessary to maintain community standards.
  • Thumbnail Moderation: Thumbnails may be reviewed for appropriateness. If rejected, you may resubmit with suitable changes.
  • Event Deletion: Events violating guidelines may be removed without prior warning. The creator will be notified with a reason if applicable.

3. Event Participation

  • Be Respectful: Treat all event participants with respect. Harassment, bullying, or discriminatory behavior is strictly prohibited.
  • Stay on Topic: Ensure your interactions are relevant to the event at hand. Off-topic or disruptive behavior may result in removal from the event.
  • Report Issues: If you encounter any inappropriate content or behavior, report it to a moderator for review.

4. General Conduct

  • No Spam or Self-Promotion: Avoid spamming events or using them solely for self-promotion unless explicitly permitted by the event creator.
  • Privacy and Safety: Do not share personal information in event chat or media. Keep conversations safe and relevant to the event theme.

5. Enforcement and Penalties

  • Warnings and Penalties: Users violating these guidelines may receive a warning, have their events removed, or face suspension from SkunkApp depending on the severity of the offense.
  • Appeal Process: If you feel a decision was made in error, you may reach out to support for review.

By creating or participating in an event on SkunkApp, you agree to adhere to these guidelines. Thank you for helping us keep SkunkApp’s community safe, enjoyable, and inclusive!
